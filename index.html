About Company

We have been in food industry for more than 40 years, serving jaggery to all over TamilNadu. After gaining momentum and hearing customer’s demand on organic and pure jaggery powder, now we have stepped into the beautiful organic world.

We focus on offering our customers only the pure jaggery powder made from farm picked sugarcanes around TamilNadu. We assure you, the jaggery powder is made with zero chemicals and we purely rely on customer's feedback and we know the customers rely on quality. As we understood this whole trust chain, we are proud to stay in market with our product which is created as pure as we could. Enough of our words, let our product speak on its own!!
